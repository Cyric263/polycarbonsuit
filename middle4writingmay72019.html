May 2nd, 2019 - Reflections On Forgiveness

[" Forgiveness is the key to happiness...

The unforgiving mind is full of fear and offers love no room to be itself; no place where it can spread its wings in peace and soar above the turmoil of the world.

What can an unforgiving mind perceive but its damnation?

What can it behold except all its sins are real?

The unforgiving mind sees no mistakes but only sins. Yet it regards its judgment of the world irreversible, and does not see it has condemned itself to this despair.

Forgiveness is acquired. It is not inherent in the mind, which cannot sin. Learn how to give and receive forgiveness - this is the release from hell.

The unforgiving mind does not believe giving and receiving are the same.

Forgiveness is the key to happiness.

I will awaken from the dream that I am mortal, fallible and full of sin, and know I am the perfect Son of God. "]
